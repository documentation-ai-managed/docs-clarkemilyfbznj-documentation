## Overview

Get started with Clarkemilyfbznj in minutes. This guide walks you through account creation, space setup, adding your first document, and basic navigation. Follow these steps to organize your project documentation effectively.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Prerequisites: You need a valid email address and a modern browser like Chrome or Firefox. No prior setup required.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up quickly to access your documentation dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to `https://clarkemilyfbznj.com/signup` and click **Create Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and verify via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Select your role (Developer, Writer, or Admin) and agree to the terms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up a New Documentation Space

Organize your docs into dedicated spaces for projects or teams.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Interface" icon="globe">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space** > Enter name like "My Project Docs" > Select visibility (Public/Private) > **Create**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="CLI Tool" icon="terminal">
    Install the CLI first:
    ```bash
    npm install -g @clarkemilyfbznj/cli
    ```
    Then run:
    ```bash
    clarkemilyfbznj space create "My Project Docs" --private
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Add Your First Document

Create and publish your initial MDX page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Document** and name it `quickstart.mdx`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Frontmatter and Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use this structure:
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,YAML">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: Quickstart Guide
    description: Your first doc.
    ---
    
    ## Hello World
    
    Welcome to Clarkemilyfbznj!
    ````
    
    ```yaml
    title: Quickstart Guide
    description: Your first doc.
    ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Click **Preview** to check rendering, then **Publish** to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Use `{title}` and `{description}` in frontmatter for SEO. Always escape special characters like `<` and `>` with backticks in prose.
</Callout>
